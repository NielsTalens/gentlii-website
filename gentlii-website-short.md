# Making product thinking easy

Gentlii helps product teams make better decisions by structuring thinking and reducing cognitive load.

Product Thinking takes time
Most teams don’t have it
Gentlii creates that space.

---

## The suite

- **Foundations** → define the product  
- **Product Guard** → keep it aligned  
- **Feature Validator** → validate every idea  

---

# Gentlii Foundations

### Why
Strong product decisions require strong foundations.  
In reality, those foundations are often missing, outdated, or fragmented.

Without this, every decision becomes a guess.

---
Gentlii Foundations creates a structured, living product definition. It organizes your product into a fixed set of core documents.
Together, these form a coherent system that defines:
- Why the product exists  
- Who it serves  
- What it should do  
- How decisions are made  

When these elements are aligned, every new idea can be evaluated with clarity and consistency.
This is where Gentlii Feature Validation takes the stage.

### How
Drop in your existing documents.  
Gentlii structures, connects, and highlights gaps.

No invention. Just clarity.

---

# Gentlii Product Guard

### Why
Product definitions are not static. Without validation, misalignment goes unnoticed and building the wrong things become a great risk.

### How

Gentlii Product Guard continuously validates alignment across all product definition elements.
This makes gaps, conflicts, and inconsistencies  as the product evolves.

Whenever something changes in the product definition, Product Guard evaluates the impact.
This ensures that the product remains coherent as it evolves.

---

# Gentlii Feature Validator

### Why

Ideas are often judged by intuition. This leads to inconsistency.
Gentlii Feature Validator replaces intuition with structured evaluation.

---

### What
Gentlii Feature Validator checks whether feature requests align with the full product definition.

Evaluates every idea against the full product definition.
Fast decisions often reduce quality.  
Careful decisions often take too long.

Gentlii Feature Validator removes that trade-off.


Every idea gets the same structured evaluation.  
Clear reasoning. No guesswork.
