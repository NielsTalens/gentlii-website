# making creating products easy

Gentlii is a product thinking suite that helps product professionals achieve higher-quality decisions by reducing cognitive load and enabling structured thinking.

One of the characteristics of Product Thinking is that it actually requires thinking. And thinking takes head space and time. And to be honest, taking time to think or re-think product decisions is hard. And most environments do not allow it.

Gentlii is here to support and facilitate Product Thinking.

The suite consists of:

- Foundations: helps defining and maintaining a clear, structured product definition.
- Product Guard: validates alignment between all product definition elements
- Feature Validator: checks if feature requests align with the product definition

# Gentlii Foundations

## Why it exists

Strong product decisions require strong foundations.  
In reality, those foundations are often missing, outdated, or fragmented.

- Strategy lives in a slide deck no one revisits  
- Business rationale is implicit instead of explicit  
- Product vision is vague or disconnected  
- User understanding is scattered across notes and assumptions  

And most importantly:  
there is rarely time to properly create or maintain them.

Yet without these foundations, every new idea becomes a guess.

---

## Why it matters

To build better products with less effort, decisions need something to be validated against.

Every feature, improvement, or idea should connect to:
- Strategic goals  
- Business rationale  
- User needs  
- Desired outcomes  

Without that connection, teams drift into:
- Reactive decision-making  
- Feature accumulation  
- Misalignment between intent and execution  

Gentlii Foundations ensures that every decision has a clear reference point.

---

## What it is

Gentlii Foundations creates a structured, living product definition.

It organizes your product into a fixed set of core documents:

- Strategy  
- Product Vision  
- Jobs-to-be-Done  
- Product Charter  
- Feedback  

Together, these form a coherent system that defines:
- Why the product exists  
- Who it serves  
- What it should do  
- How decisions are made  

When these elements are aligned, every new idea can be evaluated with clarity and consistency.
This is where Gentlii Feature Validation takes the stage.

---

## How it works

Creating this level of clarity manually takes time most teams don’t have.

Gentlii Foundations reduces that effort.

You provide whatever information is available—documents, notes, ideas, or fragments.  
Gentlii structures, connects, and completes this into a coherent product definition.

It doesn’t invent but organizes, clarifies, and highlights gaps and contradiction.

---

## More than validation

The result is not just something to validate ideas against.

It becomes a powerful communication tool:
- Aligns teams across functions  
- Clarifies direction for stakeholders  
- Makes trade-offs explicit  
- Reduces ambiguity in discussions  

It turns product thinking from something implicit and time-consuming into something structured, accessible, and continuous.

# Gentlii Product Guard

## Why it exists

Product definitions are not static.

- Strategies evolve  
- Budgets shift  
- New technologies emerge  
- External events reshape priorities  

Over time, even well-defined foundations start to drift.

What was once aligned becomes inconsistent:
- Strategy no longer matches execution  
- Vision diverges from actual features  
- Decisions are made on outdated assumptions  

Without continuous validation, misalignment goes unnoticed and building the wrong things become a great risk.

---

## What it does

Gentlii Product Guard continuously validates alignment across all product definition elements.

It checks whether:
- Strategy, business cases,vision, principles and execution still connect  
- New inputs fit within existing foundations  
- Changes introduce contradictions or gaps  

Instead of relying on manual reviews, Product Guard makes alignment visible.

---

## How it works

Whenever something changes in the product definition, Product Guard evaluates the impact.

It identifies:
- Misalignment between documents  
- Missing connections between decisions and goals  
- Conflicts introduced by new information  

This ensures that the product remains coherent as it evolves.

---

## Why it matters

Alignment is not a one-time activity.  
It requires continuous attention.

Gentlii Product Guard helps teams:
- Detect misalignment early  
- Maintain consistency over time  
- Prevent slow drift into incoherence  

It keeps the product definition trustworthy, even as everything around it changes.

# Gentlii Feature Validator

## Why it exists

Even with strong product foundations, evaluating new ideas remains difficult.

Decisions are often based on:
- Gut feeling  
- Personal preference  
- Incomplete context  

This leads to inconsistency:
- Similar ideas are judged differently  
- Trade-offs are unclear  
- Alignment with strategy is assumed, not verified  

Gentlii Feature Validator replaces intuition with structured evaluation.

---

## What it does
Gentlii Feature Validator checks whether feature requests align with the full product definition.

Every idea is evaluated against:
- Product Vision  
- Jobs-to-be-Done  
- Product Charter  
- Feedback  

The core question is always:

> How well does this idea align with our overall product definition?

For each element, it shows:
- Alignment score  
- Where it fits  
- Where it conflicts  
- What is missing  

If alignment is weak, it answers:
- *What would make this a 5 out of 5?*

---

## How it works

Once the product foundations are in place, every new idea can be validated dynamically.

Each feature request receives:
- A structured evaluation across all product elements  
- Clear reasoning instead of opinion  
- Consistent scoring across ideas  

This makes trade-offs explicit and decisions comparable.

---

## Why it matters

Fast decisions often reduce quality.  
Careful decisions often take too long.

Gentlii Feature Validator removes that trade-off.

It helps teams:
- Make faster decisions without losing rigor  
- Justify why something should or should not be built  
- Stay aligned with strategy and product intent  

It saves time for product teams and brings clarity to everyone involved.

---

## For everyone, not just product teams

Validation should not be limited to product managers.

By making this accessible:
- Stakeholders understand decisions  
- Requesters receive clear feedback  
- Discussions shift from opinion to alignment  

Every idea is treated equally and every decision is grounded in the same logic.
