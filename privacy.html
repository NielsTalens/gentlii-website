Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-20

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gentlii. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This website is designed to provide information about our product. We do not actively collect personal data from visitors.

2. Data collection

We do not:

  • Collect personal data through forms
  • Use analytics tools such as Google Analytics
  • Track visitors across sessions
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes

3. Server logs

Our hosting provider may automatically log basic technical information when you visit the website, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Date and time of access

This data is used for security and technical purposes only and is not used to identify visitors.

4. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

6. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since we do not actively collect personal data, these rights will generally not apply in practice.
